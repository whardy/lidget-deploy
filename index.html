Our Mission

Lidget is more than just a fidget spinner. Our product is designed to challenge people to create, customize, and innovate by providing unique fidget tools that challenge the status quo. At our core we inspire builders. By providing tools to understand the physics behind a Fidget Spinner and then build it into their Lego creations.
